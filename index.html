AI Signals

More signals. Better deals.

Each signal tells part of the story. Stacked together, they tell you exactly how motivated a seller is likely to be.

High impact

Tax Delinquency

Years overdue, total owed, attorney referral status. Longer delinquency = higher seller urgency.

High impact

Absentee Ownership

Situs vs. mailing address mismatch. Out-of-state owners are statistically 3× more likely to sell below market.

Medium impact

Code Violations

Open municipal violations sourced from county records. Duration and severity both weighted.

Medium impact

Probate Filing

Estate filings indicate inherited properties — heirs often prefer a fast sale to a lengthy process.

Supporting signal

Vacant Indicator

Mailing address + utility patterns used to estimate vacancy likelihood.

Supporting signal

Ownership Duration

Long-term holders with distress signals have more equity — and more room to negotiate.
